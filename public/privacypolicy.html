Privacy Policy

You have the right to be forgotten, and can delete all saved data at any time. We do not track any sensitive or identifiable data.

You are in full control since the data is only saved on your device. Ordfejd solely use what could be considered as essential for the game to function properly.

We do receive data from you as part of the communication connection itself through the standard electronic greeting between your device and our server.

Ordfejd is completely free, without restrictions, ads or in-game purchases as the app has the focus on being fun, educational and challenging to your vocabulary.

Child Safety

The vocabulary contain about 15000 unique words with a length of five letters.

The words have been fetched from the Swedish Historical Wordlist (SAOLhist) published by the Swedish Academy and University of Gothenburg.

It's not possible to interact with other players through the app, nor do we allow users to register accounts.
